Cherry lemonade recipe


Ingredients

- 1/2 cup chopped cherries
- 1/2 cup lime juice
- 1 teaspoon of honey
- 1/4 teaspoon fennel seed powder

Preparation: Blend the chopped cherries and a little water into a blender. Pour it into a glass and add honey, lime juice and fennel seed powder. Mix well and serve.
