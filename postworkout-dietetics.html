Coconut Juice Recipe


Ingredients

- 300ml coconut water
- 2 tablespoons chopped coconut

Preparation

Add chopped coconut into the coconut juice. Refrigerate it before serving.

Cherry Lemon Juice

The natural antioxidant and anti-inflammatory qualities of cherries can relief post-exercise injury and can lead to a much faster muscle recovery. The lemons in lemonade can provide Vitamin C for a good immune system and prevent illness.

×

Cherry Lemon Recipe


Ingredients

- 1/2 cup chopped cherries
- 1/2 cup lime juice
- 1 teaspoon of honey
- 1/4 teaspoon fennel seed powder

Preparation

Blend the chopped cherries and a little water into a blender. Pour it into a glass and add honey, lime juice and fennel seed powder. Mix well and serve.

Watermelon Energizer

The drink is full of natural sugars and potent with Vitamin A and C. Not only that, it is also an electrolyte balancer, which can help in post-exercise recovery.

×

Watermelon Energizer Recipe


Ingredients

- 1 cup watermelon
- 1 cup coconut water
- A pinch of pink Himalayan salt
- 1 tablespoon of lime juice

Preparation

Blend the watermelon into a blender and strain the seeds. Pour it into a glass and add coconut juice, lime juice and pink Himalayan salt. Mix well and serve.

Peanute Butter Banana Recipe


Ingredients

- 2 large bananas
- 1/2 cup chopped apple
- 1 tablespoon of honey
- 1/2 cup milk or water

Preparation

Wash the bananas and pat them dry. Peel the bananas and apple and cut them into sizable chunks. Blend the fruit chunks in a blender along with milk or water. If the mixture is too thick, add more milk or water. Serve when finished.
